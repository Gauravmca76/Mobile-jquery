Online Application For Examination

Students should fill this form online and submit the printout of the acknowledgement to the college.

Your IP Address is recorded! Any mischief with the data of other students will attract actions as per IT Act.

KT Students to Note:

Only Students who had appeared for the Examinations in month of Second Half, 2019 and failed in some subjects can submit their form by pressing KT button below.

Fail students of previous examinations who are appearing after a gap (who had not appeared for Second Half, 2019 exam in failed subjects) have to submit conventional hard copy form to their colleges.

Students appearing for examinations of a particular semester for the first time should click Regular Button and Students who wish to apply for their failed subjects should click KT button.
